Privacy Policy for Stam Sofer

Last updated: January 2026

Introduction

Stam Sofer ("we" or "the app") is an open-source application designed to help scribes (Sofer Stam) edit and manage their texts. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Google User Data

Our app accesses your Google Drive to allow you to save and edit your documents directly from the app. Here is how we handle your data:

  • Access: The app requests access to your Google Drive files to list, open, and save files managed by the app.
  • Use: We use this access solely to display your files within the app and save your edits back to your Drive.
  • Storage: The app does not store your files on any external servers. Files are processed locally on your device and synchronized directly with Google Drive.
  • Sharing: We do not share, sell, or transfer your Google user data to any third parties or advertising tools.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository.
